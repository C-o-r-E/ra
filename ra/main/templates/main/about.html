About the Space

The idea

Helios aims to provide the tools and facilities that can help people make things. We wanted to provide a place that won’t have bothered neighbors or family members when power tools are running. A place that is safe to work with heavy equipment and that can handle fumes/particles that would normally stink up your home.

The location

Helios is 5 minutes away from the Place Saint-Henri metro station at 137 Saint-Ferdinand. The space is industrially zoned with concrete floors and brick wall. This means we have less restrictions when it comes to noise and heavy equipment. We wanted to be sure that the location doesn't restrict the projects that can take place. For example, an ideal place for a welding station is next to the brick wall.

À PROPOS

L'atelier

Helios est situé à 5 minutes du métro Place Saint-Henri au 137 Saint-Ferdinand. C'est un espace industriel avec un plancher en béton et des murs de brique. Il y a moins de restriction concernant l'utilisation de machinerie lourde et bruyante. On voulait être certain que les projets de nos membres peuvent être complétés à Helios. Par exemple, l'endroit idéal pour la station de soudure à arc sera à côté du mur de brique.
