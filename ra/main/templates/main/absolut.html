MakerFest Challenge

@HeliosMakerspace

Sponsored by Absolut

What is this challenge?

This is an open challenge to the public in Montreal. Each entrant team will receive $200 towards supplies and product to build an original and creative contraption. You have from now until June 11th to build your contraption at Helios Makerspace. Then the Showcase is at Apt.200 on June 12th.

What's in it for you?

  • Exposure at the event with notable Canada media, celebrities, and the makers community
  • Absolut will promote you and your work through their social media channels
  • 200.00$ to cover the costs of building the contraption
  • A potential for one team member to win the grand prize trip to Toronto along with a cash prize of $1,000
  • VIP access to the Apt. 200 event for participants
