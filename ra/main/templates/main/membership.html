How do I get a membership

Simply show up during any of our opening hours (see Schedule) and it will be our pleasure to walk you through the process.

Why become a member

Membership helps the makerspace be sustainable. With your contribution, it helps us pay the rent, the internet, repairing and purchasing tools.

It also gives you access during the week to the workshop.

Being a member also means that you can decide the direction where the makerspace is going. Thus, every member have a voting right during general assembly and annual assembly.

And of course, as a member, you get to use the tools and equipment in the workshop. To be extra safe, some equipment require an introduction before a member can use it.

This includes:

  • Power tools: table saw, chop saw, drill press, grinder
  • Handheld tools: drill, sander, orbital sander, dremel
  • Electronics: oscilloscope, signal generator, computers
  • And many more things that we didn't list (like a fridge, free Wi-Fi, electronics parts, wood, etc...)

Basic Code of Conduct

  1. Maintain a safe environment for all members
  2. Clean after you are done
  3. Use reasonably the resources in the space
  4. Respect the volunteers, staff, members and guests of the makerspace
